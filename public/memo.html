Your turn.
Open the app now.

Pick any prompt below, copy it, and paste it into the Gentle Parent chat. See what it says.

Try it yourself. Open the app, paste a prompt, and notice how it responds — real, warm, practical.

💡 Scenario 1 — The Morning Struggle
Type this in the app:
My toddler throws a massive tantrum every morning before school. Nothing I do helps. What should I do?
👀

Look for: Calm, step-by-step strategies — not generic advice. Notice how it acknowledges your feelings before jumping to solutions.

🌙 Scenario 2 — Bedtime Battles
Type this in the app:
My 5-year-old refuses to sleep alone. She screams until I stay. I'm exhausted. How do I fix this gently?
👀

Look for: Age-appropriate context — what's developmentally normal versus what needs attention. Practical steps, not platitudes.

💔 Scenario 3 — Tough Conversations
Type this in the app:
How do I explain to my 8-year-old that their grandparent just passed away? I don't know what words to use.
👀

Look for: Emotional depth — actual phrases you can use tonight, not just theory. This is where it shines.

🤝 Scenario 4 — Teen Connection
Type this in the app:
My teenager barely talks to me anymore. Every conversation turns into an argument. How do I reconnect without it feeling forced?
👀

Look for: Strategies that respect the teen's perspective AND yours. Real advice from someone who actually gets it.
